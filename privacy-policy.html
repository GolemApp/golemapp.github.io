Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4th, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Golem, an AI-driven app designed to transform everyday activities into engaging quests. We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our app.

2. Data Controller

Golem is created by Avocado Technologies UG (haftungsbeschränkt), a company based in Berlin, Germany.

3. Types of Personal Data Collected

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and profile information.
  • Activity Data: Information on the activities you complete within the app, including timestamps and completion status.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Location Data: GPS location data to enhance the gamification experience.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use our app, including interaction data and preferences.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: To enhance user experience and track app performance.
4. Purposes of Data Collection

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: Ensure the app functions as intended and provide updates.
  • To Personalize User Experience: Tailor quests and activities based on user preferences and past interactions.
  • To Improve the App: Analyze usage data to improve app features and performance.
  • To Communicate with Users: Send notifications, updates, and promotional materials (with user consent).
  • To Ensure Security: Monitor and prevent fraudulent activities and security breaches.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: Ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
5. Data Storage and Retention

We store your personal data securely on our servers located within the European Union. We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Upon request, we will delete or anonymize your data, subject to legal and operational requirements.

6. User Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation on the processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where we are relying on consent to process your data.
7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Data Processing with Third Parties

Effective February 10, 2025, Avocado Technologies UG (haftungsbeschränkt) has entered into a Data Processing Addendum with OpenAI, through their European Union entity, OpenAI Ireland Ltd. This agreement specifies how OpenAI processes data on behalf of Avocado Technologies UG to provide and enhance our services, ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws and regulations.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

By using the Golem app, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and storage of your personal data as described herein.
