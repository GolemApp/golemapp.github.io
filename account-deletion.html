Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the Golem app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Golem app.
  2. Go to Settings > Scroll to the bottom of the screen > Delete Account.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion.

Once deleted, all associated data will be removed as per our privacy policy. This action is irreversible.

For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
